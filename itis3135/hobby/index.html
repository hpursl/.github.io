About

Gaming has been a cornerstone of my life for over 16 years. It all started with a Nintendo DS given to me by my cousin and a curiosity that quickly turned into a lifelong passion. From long RPG adventures to fast FPS battles, I've immersed myself in almost every genre you can imagine. What keeps me coming back to gaming? It's the blend of strategy, storytelling, and skill that each game demands. Whether I'm cracking complex puzzles in a simulation game or coordinating strategies in a team-based shooter, I thrive on challenges that push me to think creatively and stay sharp. When I'm not gaming, you can probably find me tinkering with my PC setup, diving into game lore, or crafting web experiences like this one. I also enjoy experimenting with CSS to make websites pop, bringing the same level of detail and care to coding as I do to gaming. This is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way of life. Gaming has shaped my perspective, taught me resilience, and connected me with amazing communities and people.

Reviews & Ratings

Borderlands 2 Borderlands 2 is a vibrant and chaotic first-person shooter that masterfully blends RPG elements with addictive loot mechanics. Set on the planet Pandora, the game offers a quirky, humor-filled narrative featuring memorable characters like the villainous Handsome Jack and the lovable Claptrap. Its cel-shaded art style and diverse environments make it visually distinct. ★★★★★
Sekiro Shadows Die Twice Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is a masterful action-adventure game by FromSoftware, set in a beautifully crafted, fantastical version of feudal Japan. The game stands out for its intense, skill-based combat system, which revolves around precise timing, parrying, and a unique posture mechanic. Unlike other FromSoftware titles, Sekiro emphasizes stealth and mobility, with tools like a grappling hook adding verticality to exploration and combat. ★★★★★
Mirrors Edge Catalyst Mirror's Edge Catalyst is a first-person parkour game that serves as a reboot and prequel to the original Mirror's Edge. It expands on the free-running mechanics with an open-world design, allowing players to explore the futuristic City of Glass. The movement system is fluid and satisfying, offering a sense of freedom as you vault, wall-run, and slide across rooftops. ★★★★☆
Lies of P Lies of P is a dark, gothic reimagining of the classic Pinocchio tale, blending it with the challenging gameplay of a Soulslike. Set in a grim, 19th-century-inspired world, the game offers a compelling story of a puppet's quest to become human, paired with methodical combat mechanics and intricate boss battles. ★★★★☆
Final Fantasy VII Remake is a stunning reimagining of the beloved 1997 classic, focusing on the Midgar segment of the original game. It combines breathtaking visuals, a reworked real-time combat system with tactical elements, and an expanded storyline that delves deeper into the characters and world. ★★★★★
